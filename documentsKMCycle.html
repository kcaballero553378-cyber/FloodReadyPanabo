Knowledge Management
The KM Cycle

This portal applies the full Knowledge Management cycle to ensure information is effectively captured, stored, shared, and applied for community resilience.

📥
Capture
Citizen incident reports, hazard data, and community observations are collected through online forms and submissions.
🗄️
Storage
Data is organized into structured databases, documents, and categorized forms for easy retrieval and reference.
📢
Sharing
Advisories, guides, evacuation plans, and announcements are disseminated to citizens and partner agencies.
⚙️
Application
Knowledge is used for improved preparedness, informed decision-making, and faster disaster response.
